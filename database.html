League of Legends is popular because there are so many different champions that player may choose. Although they all have different but somehow designated role in team, we still can't determine which champion is absolutely stronger than others. So all champions are welcomed by certain group of people, and this balance of champions definitely make this game more fun. However, in order to win the game, we need to make some good arrangment in a team, so that each one has its own role. Also, it is crucial that we know well about out opponent. Thus, we have to know who is our best Partners and who is our worst encounters.
